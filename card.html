Eevee

Eevee has two forms: the partner Eevee and Gigantamax Eevee.

  • Scientific Name: E. evolutionis
  • Average Length: 30.5cm
  • Average Lifespan: 20 - 40 years
  • Habitat: Temperate forests; Urban areas

Eevee, in its standard Normal-type form, appears to be a brown fox with long ears and a white ruff of fur on its neck. As stated above, its genome is unstable, thus enabling it to evolve into various different forms, depending on conditions.
